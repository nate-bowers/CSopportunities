Introduction

Welcome to my project about helping students interested in computer science find opportunities! I am passionate about helping children explore the exciting world of computer science and programming.

My Mission

The main goal of this project is to provide a comprehensive resource for parents, educators, and students to discover various computer science programs, camps, competitions, and online courses tailored specifically for kids.

I believe that easy, organized access to computer science not only helps children develop important problem-solving and critical thinking skills but also sparks their creativity and curiosity in the digital age. This set of opportunities is taylored to the San Francisco Bay Area.
